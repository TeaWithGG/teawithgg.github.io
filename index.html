About

There is a kind of person
who walks into a room
and immediately reads it.

I have always been that person. Not because I was taught to — but because attention is the most natural thing I do. I sense what hides beneath facades. I read emotions that people don't say out loud. And I match myself to the room not to blend in — but to make something real happen.

I grew up between Amritsar and London — two cities, two cultures, two entirely different ways of moving through the world. That distance gave me two lenses: one that sees with precision, one that sees with warmth. Both live in everything I create.

A debate judge — someone whose job was to find weakness — told me I had the precision of a natural anchor and the instincts of a real journalist. I didn't celebrate. I got to work.

I'm a journalist interning at The Tribune, a cinematographer who has directed two complete works entirely solo, a photographer who shoots silence, a public speaker who has held stages, and a visual storyteller who believes every frame, every word, and every room has a story waiting to be told right.

My thought process is simple — I don't give up and I don't take no for an answer. If there's a solution I find it. If there isn't — I create one. I believe in fighting for the truth even when I'm the only one fighting for it. I transform empathy into action. I thrive in the space where craft meets urgency.Im not fit to one role im fluid, like my craft

Amritsar is not just where I live — it is what I see through. A city of extraordinary cultural and spiritual richness that lives in every street, every voice, every evening light. It shows up in my photography, my journalism, my cinematography. Amritsar is in the texture of everything I make.

Philosophy
यत् भावो — तत् भवति
"You become what you believe"

This is not a quote I carry — it is a compass I live by. Every frame I shoot, every word I write, every stage I stand on is built on this foundation: that belief precedes reality. That what you hold in your mind with enough conviction, you will eventually hold in your hands.

I believe in fighting for the truth even when I'm the only one fighting for it. I believe that empathy without action is just observation. I believe that the most powerful stories are the ones told with both precision and heart — and that these are not opposites.

01
Truth is non-negotiable

Fact-oriented. No-nonsense. I will fight for the truth even when I'm the only one in the room who still believes it matters.

02
Empathy becomes action

I sense what lives beneath the surface — and I don't leave it there. I transform what I feel into something that moves, reaches, and changes.

03
There is always a way

I don't give up. I don't take no for an answer when a solution exists. And when it doesn't — I find one. This is not stubbornness. It is commitment.

The First Frame

I picked up a camera and discovered I had been seeing in macro all along. Every shot an emotion. Every frame a decision. Beauty doesn't announce itself — it waits.

My photography is 90% macro — the world in extreme close-up. A bee on a mustard flower at golden hour. The universe inside a human iris. A wet leaf on green grass. I don't hunt for beauty. I wait for it to reveal itself — and then I witness it. @clickswithgg on Instagram became the home for this work.
